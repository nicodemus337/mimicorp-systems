About Mimicorp Systems

Mimicorp Systems is a working platform for research, media, and applied systems thinking. The site is organized around the idea that governance, ecology, and lived experience are inseparable in practice, and that public work should be able to move between concept, field observation, and production without losing rigor.

Research

Dissertation development and long-form writing on governance, time, ecological systems, performance, and judgment.

Production

Media work that documents systems from the inside through field notes, audio, and observational formats grounded in place.

Orientation

The method behind the work is cross-disciplinary but not diffuse. It treats institutions, land, and infrastructure as overlapping realities and asks how authority is made to appear credible in ordinary life.

Rather than separating analysis from experience, Mimicorp Systems uses the friction between them as a source of knowledge. The result is work that is both conceptual and field-attentive.
