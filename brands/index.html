Legacy Brands in New Iberia, Louisiana

mimicorp labs is currently supporting a group of legacy brands rooted in New Iberia, Louisiana. These are local, independent small businesses with long-standing ties to land, trade, hospitality, and regional life. The aim is to package them clearly without stripping away the history and familiarity that make them trusted in the first place.

Legacy Brand / Ranch

Gonsoulin Land and Cattle

A local ranch business with deep roots in South Louisiana, centered on grass-fed, grass-finished beef and direct relationships with families who want to know where their food comes from.

Packaged as a legacy brand, it carries more than product quality. It represents continuity, stewardship, and a recognizable local standard of trust tied to land, animal care, and multigenerational work.

What Makes Them Legacy Brands

What links these businesses is not just category. It is inheritance, continuity, and local recognition. Each one operates as an independent small business with a public identity shaped by long-term relationships, regional memory, and work that people in New Iberia already know firsthand.

The branding work therefore focuses on articulation rather than invention: making each business legible online and in public-facing materials while keeping the voice grounded in the place, people, and standards that built the reputation to begin with.
