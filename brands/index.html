Active Brand Work

Brands in Active Development

Mimicorp Systems is currently supporting brand work across agriculture, supply, and outfitting. These projects share a common concern with place, credibility, and how a business speaks clearly from the life it actually lives.

Ranch / Grassfed Beef

Gonsoulin Land and Cattle

A multigenerational Louisiana ranch centered on locally produced grass-fed, grass-finished, antibiotic-free, and growth hormone-free beef, with direct sales, bundles, custom beef, and local market presence.

The brand carries both agricultural stewardship and direct-to-consumer trust, connecting land, animal care, and food quality in a highly visible public voice.

Lumber / Building Supply

Gonsoulin Price Supply and Lumber

A regional supply business rooted in service to builders and families across Iberia and St. Martin Parish, emphasizing practical inventory, fast quoting, and dependable customer support.

The work here is about clarity, usefulness, and trust: a brand presence that feels grounded in trade, responsiveness, and long-standing local relationships.

Outfitting / Guided Hunts

Teche Lake Outfitters

A South Louisiana outfitter offering small, personal guided upland bird hunts on family land shaped by generations of stewardship along Bayou Teche.

Its brand position is less about volume than experience: honest hunting, true hospitality, managed fields and woods, and a deliberately personal approach to place-based tradition.

Shared Throughline

Across all three brands, the challenge is similar: to build public language that is not generic to the category but specific to the land, the work, and the people behind it. The point is not polish for its own sake. It is coherence between operations, story, and trust.
