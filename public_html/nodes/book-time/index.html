What This Time Can Do

Bring in the idea before it is fully formed.

You do not need a polished brief. This can be a first conversation about a site that no longer fits, an email or account issue, a form that keeps breaking, a tech problem that keeps bouncing around your to-do list, or a project that needs structure.

  • Name the actual problem instead of circling around it.
  • Figure out the next move, the right structure, or the right scope.
  • Decide whether it makes sense to keep working together.
