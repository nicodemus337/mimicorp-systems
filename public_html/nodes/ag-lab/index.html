Ag Lab

An experimental agricultural lab linking ranch operations, USDA VAPG funding, and ecological research.

Research Field Systems Prototyping

Purpose

A working research environment

Ag Lab is a working research environment where agriculture, technology, and storytelling intersect. The lab operates within real agricultural systems, beginning with ranching operations like Gonsoulin Land and Cattle and extending into broader ecological observation across working landscapes in South Louisiana.

Why It Matters

One of the central catalysts for this work is support from the USDA Value Added Producer Grant. These investments support marketing systems, stronger production and processing capacity, and ecological observation through tools such as drone imaging, soil surveys, and ecological datasets.

Feedback Loop

Marketing supports production. Production supports research. Research deepens stewardship of the land. Ag Lab exists to make that loop visible and to explore how agricultural systems can become more resilient, transparent, and ecologically grounded in the decades ahead.
