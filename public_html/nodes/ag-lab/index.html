Ag Lab

Ag Lab is an experimental space for observing and testing agricultural systems.

Research Field Systems Prototyping

Purpose

Prototype practical systems

The goal is simple: pay closer attention to how land actually works. Projects include ecological monitoring, pasture observation, drone imaging, soil analysis, and the development of lightweight sensor networks that help reveal patterns in the landscape.

Why It Matters

Some experiments succeed. Some simply generate better questions. Ag Lab exists to make those questions visible.
