Data

Data projects focus on gathering and organizing information about land and ecological systems.

Archive Measurement Mapping

Purpose

Make the system observable

Examples include soil surveys, drone imagery, pasture observations, and sensor based monitoring. The purpose is not surveillance or scale for its own sake. Instead, these datasets help reveal patterns that are otherwise difficult to see.

Why It Matters

Over time they form part of a larger ecological archive.
