Approach

Each project is unique and requires its own budget.

Some jobs are a one-call fix. Some need a few focused sessions. Some turn into a full website rebuild or a deeper systems project. The budget follows the actual work, not a canned package.

The point is to price the real shape of the job, set expectations early, and avoid selling the wrong thing.

Budgeting

What determines the budget.

  • Whether it is a quick fix, a short support project, or a larger build.
  • How many tools, accounts, or moving parts are involved.
  • How quickly the work needs to happen.
  • Whether you need strategy only, implementation, or both.

First Step

Start with a conversation.

If you are not sure what bucket the problem belongs in yet, that is fine. A short consult is the easiest way to figure out whether this is a one-off fix, a contained project, or the start of something larger.
