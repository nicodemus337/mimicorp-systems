What Mimicorp does and how the parts fit together.

Mimicorp is a software and media creation company building narrative, tools, and project infrastructure around ecological work. This page gives the fuller version of that story.

Software Media Creation Strategy Project Infrastructure

Software

Build tools that make ecological work more legible.

Mimicorp develops decision tools, archives, and digital systems that help teams organize evidence, track complexity, and move from scattered observations toward clearer action.

Media Creation

Show the work in a form people can trust.

Documentary, field storytelling, and partner-facing media help complex projects earn attention, credibility, and alignment without flattening what makes them real.

Strategy + Narrative

Turn field complexity into a coherent case.

Positioning, communication systems, and strategic framing help ecological projects become clearer to funders, partners, and stakeholders.

The Ecological Archive

The work also connects to The Ecological Archive, where Amelia serves on the Board of Directors as Secretary.
