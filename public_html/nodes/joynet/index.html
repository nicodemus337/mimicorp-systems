JoyNet

JoyNet explores the philosophical and ethical frameworks that underlie systems of belief, community, and responsibility.

Community Publishing Collaboration

Purpose

Connect people to the system

Part digital monastery, part research project, JoyNet looks at how meaning, ritual, and symbolic systems evolve alongside technology.

Why It Matters

Its work ranges from essays and sermons to experiments in networked communities and civic imagination. Where the other Mimicorp branches observe land and infrastructure, JoyNet focuses on the cultural and ethical systems that shape how people live within them.
