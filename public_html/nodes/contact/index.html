About Amelia

Software, media, and field-based storytelling built through Mimicorp.

Mimicorp is the operating platform for Amelia's software work, media practice, and ecological storytelling. It brings together digital systems, documentary thinking, and place-based observation to help complex land-based projects become more legible, more human, and easier to support.

Biography

Amelia works at the intersection of software, media, and land-based observation.

Through Mimicorp, Amelia develops software systems, documentary media, and strategic communication around ecological work. The through-line is a commitment to making complex realities easier to understand without flattening them into generic branding or abstract positioning.

Her work draws from direct observation, rural context, and long-form narrative. Some of it becomes digital infrastructure. Some of it becomes media. Some of it becomes the public language that helps a project earn trust, backing, or alignment.

Mimicorp exists as the place where those practices come together: software and media creation in service of ecological infrastructure, field intelligence, and durable project development.

Amelia's current work also includes The Ecological Archive, where she serves on the Board of Directors as Secretary.
