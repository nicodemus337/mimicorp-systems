About Amelia

Thoughtful local help for websites, digital systems, and small tech projects.

Amelia Cronan brings together digital problem-solving, design sense, communication skills, and hands-on project support. Through Mimicorp, she helps local people and small organizations make their websites, tools, and workflows easier to manage.

Website help Tech troubleshooting Creative strategy Local projects

Biography

Amelia works at the intersection of communication, technology, and practical support.

Her background spans e-commerce, nonprofit growth, campaigns, public art, agriculture, creative direction, and digital systems work. That mix makes her especially useful for projects that need both technical help and a human sense of presentation.

At Gonsoulin Land and Cattle, Amelia developed digital marketing and communications infrastructure grounded in brand storytelling, website systems, grant writing, analytics, and practical day-to-day operations.

Through Mimicorp, she also leads creative projects, websites, and digital support rooted in South Louisiana. The work includes local campaigns, nonprofit consulting, media production, and systems help for small projects that need more clarity and less friction.

Amelia's current work also includes The Ecological Archive, which she co-founded and where she serves on the Board of Directors as Secretary, helping shape governance, organizational growth, and long-term mission alignment.

Credentials

Grounded in real-world communication, technical curiosity, and long experience with complex projects.

Amelia brings 10+ years of experience in digital marketing, communication systems, project organization, grant development, and stakeholder-facing work across commercial, nonprofit, political, and creative sectors.

Her formal background includes an M.A. in Political Science from the University of Washington, a B.A. in Political Science and German from Loyola University New Orleans, computer science and electrical engineering coursework at the University of Louisiana at Lafayette, and CompTIA A+ certification.

She works fluently across English and German, with strong Cajun French, French, and Russian language abilities, and brings formal music training in piano and guitar into media, educational, and narrative work when it strengthens the message.
