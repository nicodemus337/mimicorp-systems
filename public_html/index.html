About Mimicorp

A research lab and creative studio with a serious systems practice.

Mimicorp brings together ecological research, public-facing art, and digital systems work. Some projects look like field inquiry, some look like cultural production, and some look like infrastructure for real organizations. The point is not to separate those instincts, but to let them sharpen each other.

What it feels like

Rigorous, intentional, aesthetically alive, and practical enough to survive contact with real conditions.

Who it helps

Researchers, artists, land-based projects, cultural institutions, and organizations that need both depth and execution.

Why People Reach Out

Three reasons serious projects usually end up here.

Research frame

A project needs stronger language, documentation, evidence structure, or a more coherent research container.

Public expression

The work is meaningful but hard to communicate, and it needs a sharper artistic or narrative form in public.

Systems build

The infrastructure is lagging behind the ambition, and the digital side needs to be designed, rebuilt, or brought into alignment.

Work Together

Bring Mimicorp in when the research, expression, or infrastructure needs to get sharper.

This can look like a research collaboration, a documentary or public-facing studio project, a website or archive build, or the operational system that has become too important to keep improvising around.

You do not need to flatten the idea before reaching out. If the work is serious and needs thought, form, and execution, it is worth a conversation.
