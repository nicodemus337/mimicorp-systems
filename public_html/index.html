About Mimicorp

A software and media creation company making ecological projects more legible and investable.

Mimicorp is a software and media creation company focused on helping ecological infrastructure move from field reality to durable execution. The business combines software tools, documentary storytelling, strategic narrative, and partner-facing design to make complex land-based projects easier to understand, support, and finance.

Mission

Mimicorp develops the narrative, evidence, and strategic infrastructure that helps ecological projects attract capital, align stakeholders, and move from concept to durable execution.

Vision

Mimicorp envisions a future where ecological infrastructure is investable, legible, and scalable because the gap between field conditions, public understanding, and capital deployment has been closed.
