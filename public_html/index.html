About Mimicorp

A local creative-tech studio for people who want capable help without the agency theater.

Mimicorp helps local people, small businesses, and mission-driven projects with websites, digital organization, creative strategy, and tech problems that need a real person on them. The work is direct, hands-on, and built to get things moving again.

What it feels like

Thoughtful design, plain language, and less dread around the digital side of your work.

Who it helps

Small businesses, farms, artists, nonprofits, and local people who want thoughtful support without being talked down to.
