Series

Second Cutting

A field dialogue series on systems, stewardship, labor, and perception. The title comes from haymaking, where the second cutting reveals what a season is doing after the first pass. The project uses that logic as a method: return to the same ground, listen again, and notice what changed.

Focus

Practical Intelligence

Conversations with people whose knowledge is formed through maintenance, repetition, timing, and contact with consequence rather than through abstraction alone.

System Failure

Episodes that track what happens when inherited categories stop matching the world and people have to improvise judgment inside failing systems.

Land and Governance

A recurring thread connecting ecological observation, institutional language, and the performance of authority in ordinary life.

Development Notes

  • Pilot interviews are being shaped around agriculture, weather, and administrative lag.
  • Companion writing will connect audio material back to the dissertation project.
  • The long-term aim is a body of work that can function as both media and archive.
