Mimicorp

A field lab for research, systems work, and public projects.

Mimicorp is a lab where research, digital systems, media, and narrative experimentation can develop alongside each other instead of being split into separate boxes.

The lab holds work across research writing, computational interpretation, audio, documentary form, and site-based publication, making room for rigorous work that still has form and texture.
