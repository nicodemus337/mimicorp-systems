Approach

Each project is unique and requires its own budget.

Some jobs are quick fixes. Some need a few focused sessions. Some turn into a larger website or systems project. The budget depends on the actual size and shape of the work.

The point is clear expectations and a good fit, not forcing every project into the same package.

Budgeting

What determines the budget.

  • Whether it is a quick fix, a short support project, or a larger build.
  • How many tools, accounts, or moving parts are involved.
  • How quickly the work needs to happen.
  • Whether you need strategy only, implementation, or both.

First Step

Start with a conversation.

If you are not sure what kind of help you need yet, that is normal. A short consult is the easiest way to talk through the problem and figure out whether it is a quick fix, a small project, or something bigger.
