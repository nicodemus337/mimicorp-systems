Second Cutting

Second Cutting is a podcast and documentary project about land, animals, people, and the systems that connect them.

Media Field Reporting Documentary

Purpose

Editorial system for ecology

The name comes from haymaking. The second cutting of hay is often richer and more complex than the first. In the same way, these conversations look past the surface of agriculture and ask deeper questions about biology, stewardship, food systems, and the relationship between people and the land they depend on.

Why It Matters

Episodes feature ranchers, veterinarians, farmers, scientists, and others whose work sits at the intersection of ecology and everyday life. Second Cutting documents how knowledge moves through a working landscape.
