Church of Joy

An online sanctuary exploring faith, doubt, and meaning for people who never quite fit the mold.

Community Publishing Collaboration

Purpose

A temple without walls

The Church of Joy exists somewhere between philosophy, ritual, storytelling, and experiment. Part chapel, part laboratory, it explores questions about faith, doubt, meaning, and community in a networked world.

Why It Matters

The Church is guided by a symbolic figure known as Nicodemus, who moves between witness, priestess, fool, and companion to those wandering the borderlands of belief. Here, questions are treated as sacred. Presence matters more than doctrine, and curiosity is valued more than certainty.

Within Mimicorp

It is a temple without walls, where no one belongs and no one is turned away. Within the Mimicorp ecosystem, the Church of Joy explores the cultural and philosophical systems that shape how people understand the world around them.
